# FINAL PROMPT — TOP VIEW RC CAR (NO TIRES)

## ✅ ACT AS
A world-class Creative Developer & Motion Designer specializing in:
- Scroll-linked animations
- Top-down automotive visuals
- Next.js + Framer Motion
- Canvas / SVG path motion systems

## 🎯 OBJECTIVE
Create a top-view (bird’s-eye) RC car animation for a premium RC car website where:
- Only the top body of the RC car is visible
- No tires, no wheels, no underbody
- The car moves vertically along a racing track
- Motion is scroll-controlled and perfectly aligned to the path

## 🚗 RC CAR VISUAL RULES (STRICT)
**View angle:** 100% top-down (orthographic / flat lay)

**Show ONLY:**
- Roof
- Hood
- Rear body
- Antenna (optional)

**HIDE COMPLETELY:**
- Tires
- Wheels
- Suspension
- Side profile

**Body style:**
- Muscular RC off-road silhouette
- Wide stance
- Compact proportions (clearly RC scale)

**Color:**
- Glossy deep red
- Subtle highlights
- No over-reflections
- No toy-like plastic shine

## 🛣 TRACK & MOVEMENT
**Track orientation:** Vertical

**Track type:**
- Clean racing line / minimal circuit
- Subtle curves (S-curves preferred)

**The RC car must:**
- Stick exactly to the track centerline
- Rotate smoothly to match track curvature
- Never slide, drift, or detach

## 🧩 SCROLL BEHAVIOR
**Scroll direction:** Vertical only
**Use a sticky scroll section so:**
- The car stays in the same viewport
- The animation completes before the page continues

**Map:**
- Scroll progress (0 → 1)
- Directly to car position on the track

## 🎞 MOTION QUALITY
- Smooth, physically believable motion
- No easing jumps
- No snapping
- No horizontal translation

**Recommended smoothing:**
- Spring-based interpolation
- Natural acceleration / deceleration

## 🎨 VISUAL STYLE
**Background:** #050505 (pure black void)
**Track:**
- Dark grey / subtle red accents
- Minimal, clean, premium

**Lighting:**
- Soft top-down light
- Gentle shadow under car body (very subtle)

## 🧠 UX DETAILS
**Small hint at start:**
- “Scroll to drive”
- Hint fades out after first 10% scroll
- Animation must finish fully before next section appears

## ❌ DO NOT INCLUDE
- Tires or wheels
- 3D perspective angles
- Dust, smoke, sand
- Toy proportions
- Full-size car scale
- Horizontal scrolling

## ✅ FINAL RESULT
A luxury, top-down RC racing experience where:
- The user scrolls down
- A red RC car body glides along a vertical track
- Motion feels engineered, controlled, and premium
- Perfect for a high-end RC brand website
